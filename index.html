About Me

Building cities that work for everyone.

I'm an urban planner with over a decade of experience working at the intersection of policy, design, and community development. My work spans transit-oriented development, affordable housing advocacy, and climate-resilient infrastructure.

I believe the best cities are shaped by deep listening — to residents, to data, and to the rhythms of daily life. Whether leading a comprehensive plan for a mid-sized municipality or analyzing zoning reform at the state level, I bring rigor and empathy to every project.

Currently based in New York. Open to consulting engagements and collaborative research.
